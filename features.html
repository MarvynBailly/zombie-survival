Shipped core

Top-down arena loop

Fixed-timestep 60 Hz update, interpolated render, camera follow, procedural 32k×32k world generated from a noise sampler — chunked and lazily activated around the player.

Twelve base weapons

Pistol, shotgun, SMG, rocket, barrels, walls, crossbow, flamer, minigun, railgun, grenade launcher, chainsaw — slot keys 1–=.

Wave director & day cycle

Day/dusk/night phases, escalating spawn budgets, edge-based and POI-bound emitters. Blood-moon-style harder nights gated by the trader system.

Squad & bots

Up to 3 recruitable survivors (medic / engineer / soldier) with permadeath, leash AI, hold-position, and named backstories. Already wired in squad.js.

Perks (4 lanes × 6)

Gunner / Medic / Engineer / Scout. One point per day survived; unlocks reset on death. Buff registry consulted via perkMult() / perkSum().

Weather + lore

Daily forecast (clear / fog / heatwave / rain / blizzard) with speed & aggro multipliers. lore.js ships 40+ flavor fragments looted from furniture.

Sewers & bunkers

Manholes portal into instanced, deterministically-seeded dungeons (8–15 rooms). Same manhole = same layout across runs.

PocketBase leaderboard

Score, waves, kills, weapon stats submitted on death to proj_zombie_survival_scores. Graceful offline fallback.

Phase 0 — Foundations

Faction system

Five factions with an asymmetric hostility table — wildlife attacks raiders only if wired both ways. Drives target selection in updateZombies.

  • zombie → player, raider, wildlife
  • raider → player, zombie
  • cultist → player (neutral to zombies)
  • wildlife → player, zombie

Player infection

0–100% bar under HP. Decays 0.3/s when no recent source. Spitter spit adds 3%, bloater aura 0.5%/tick, rats 1% per bite. Death at 100.

Boss arena framework

Boss-on-engage: ring of indestructible walls erects around Game.bossArena, top-center health bar + nameplate, phase callbacks at HP thresholds (66%, 33%), walls drop on death.

Phase 1 — Horde behaviors

Pack flanking

When ≥5 same-faction zombies within 200px of player, 30% are deterministically tagged with a flank side and add lateral steering offset perpendicular to the player vector.

Sire call

1-in-12 walkers spawn as a sire (red shoulder cloth). On death they emit a screech FX and schedule 4 extra walker spawns over 8s.

Stampede

Groups of 8+ walkers within 4 tiles accrue momentum. At cap they sprint at ×1.6 speed for 4s and trample tier-1 walls.

A placeable-machine layer on top of the world: gather ore, sulfur, oil, brass — feed them through chains that refine, press, and assemble finished cartridges. All machine instances live on Game.machines; each is a tiny state machine with inputs / outputs / progress / recipeId.

Production chain (Phase 1 MICRO — hand-hauled)
EXTRACTION
  • Hand Mine — saltpeter / sulfur / charcoal
  • Ore Drill — iron / copper / lead
  • Pumpjack — sweet or sour crude (toxic)
▶
REFINING
  • Blast Furnace — ore → ingot, cast bullets (lead vents toxic gas)
  • Powder Mill — saltpeter + sulfur + charcoal → fast/med/slow powder
  • Refinery Tower — cracks crude into 4 named precursors (powder/casing/lube/fuel)
  • Centrifuge — purifies saltpeter/sulfur, cracks bile → acid + lye
▶
ASSEMBLY
  • Casing Press — copper → brass strip → brass casing
  • Primer Bench — compound + copper → primer caps
  • Ammo Press — casing + primer + powder + bullet → finished cartridges
  • Specialty Press — incendiary, AP, acid, capacitor rounds
Phase 2 — MIDI (automation layer)

Silo (buffer)

Bulk storage — drop any precursor in, pull it back later. 9999 cap. Buffer for night-shift production.

Foreman Console

Top-down x-ray of your foundry — live throughput per cluster, fill %, alarms when an output is full or an input is starved.

Phase 4 — TWISTS
E·02

Gremlin Baffle

Placeable defensive grate that stops gremlins (and small swarmers) from wedging into machine collision boxes.

E·01

Stench / aggro

Running machines accrue a foundry-wide foundryStenchScore — the higher it climbs, the harder zombies pull toward your factory. Stop production at night, or pay in tank rushes.

E·03

Each ammo press cycle rolls a quality tier. Better tiers = tighter spread & slightly higher damage when those cartridges are fired. Logged in foundryQualityLog.

HOMESTEAD — Bases, survival & colony UNCOMMITTED · 17 MODULES

worktree: homestead-features · base 30d1eb1

The largest single feature line — 17 self-contained modules in the worktree, each with its own integration doc under .homestead-integration/. Every module is additive: it never modifies an existing file; the integrator wires it in via a script tag plus a handful of hook calls.

Base building (cluster A–C)
A·02 / A·03 / D·02

Walls — tiers, blueprints, decay

Tier up: wood / brick / steel (HP 200 / 600 / 1500). Steel reflects bullets. Blueprints: press B to capture a 16×16 box, paste it as a ghost, click to spend scrap. Decay: 1–3% / day (×2 in rain). Hold R to repair (1 scrap = +20).

B·01

Bases / Safehouse claims

Plant a Base Flag on a clear tile to claim a safehouse (max 3, min 800px apart, effective ring 480px). Other modules (raid, RV, cameras) all hook off nearestBase().

B·02

Garden plots

Till tiles, plant seeds, water with bucket (or let rain do it), harvest crops the kitchen consumes. State on Game.gardenPlots.

B·03

Garage

Roof + 4-wall garage that refuels & repairs any vehicle parked inside (5s tick). Roof goes translucent when you enter.

C·02

Moats & pits

Dig flat trench tiles (hold-to-dig with progress meter). Non-tank zombies slow to 40%, bleed 30/s; spiked tiles deal 60 dmg on enter. Tanks immune.

C·03

Noise Box

Battery-powered decoy — pulls zombie aggro to a placed point until the battery dies. Distracts hordes away from your base for night defense.

C·04

Cameras & monitors

Place cameras on poles, view feeds from a wall-mounted monitor overlay. Both need power. Both can be destroyed.

Survival systems (cluster D–F)
B·05

Power grid

Generators drain fuel, light a powered radius. Required by Garage tools, Radio, Cameras. Damaged generators drop half-fuel as a scrap pickup.

B·06

Kitchen & cooking

Cook station consumes crops + canned goods into meals. Meals apply timed buffs (speed, sprint regen, cold resist, etc.) via applyBuff().

D·01

Trader + Blood Moon

NPC trader walks the map, opens a shop overlay. Advances a nextBloodMoonDay counter — read by Raid Night and Radio modules.

D·03

Raid Night

On a blood moon, hive bursts + brood drops swarm nearest base. HUD banner + directional arrow guide you back to the besieged claim.

D·04 / F3

Vehicles & RV

Drivable vehicles with fuel drain, repair via parts, fire DPS when crashed. RV doubles as a mobile mini-base.

E·01

Trophy room

Special zombies drop one of 8 trophies. Mount on a plinth crafted from a plinth kit. Plinth props reset per run, but mounted trophies persist forever in 'zombie-survival:trophies'.

E·02

Campfire bonding

Hold E on a campfire at dusk with a squadmate within 80 tiles → 90s pause cutscene. Everyone gets +20 morale and +15% accuracy until next day flip.

F4c → E·04

Lore: journals, screenshots, hand-drawn maps

Three subsystems: journals lootable from chests (~12%), discrete-event canvas screenshots, sketchpad consumable that snaps explored chunks. Persists across runs in 'zombie-survival:lore-meta'.

F4a

Weather (foundation)

Daily forecast: clear / fog / heatwave / rain / blizzard. Multiplier helpers consulted by player speed, sprint drain, zombie speed, aggro radius, flamer ignite. Particle pools persist between frames so visuals don't strobe.

A·05

Prefabs

Workbench tab with pre-shaped wall layouts (L, T, room, corridor). Click-paste, spend scrap, instant build.
