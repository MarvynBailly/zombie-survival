THE RAILGUN

Hold to charge. Release to vaporize a line.

A two‑second charge‑up mounted on a tripod or shouldered (slower while moving). On release: a hitscan beam that pierces every enemy in a straight line for the full length of the screen. Headshots one‑tap up to Heavy tier; staggers a Tank.

Ammo is capacitor cells — Mythic‑chest only, 6 per pickup. The risk is the charge animation: you're rooted, glowing, and audible. Miss a Spitter and you eat acid.

GRENADE LAUNCHER

Lob 40mm arcs into a pile of walkers.

Arcing projectile — aim with a faint dotted trajectory. 4‑round break‑action, ~3s reload per shell. Detonation does a 4‑tile radius. Two ammo families: HE (damage), Smoke (breaks line‑of‑sight, drops aggro).

NAIL GUN

Scrap‑powered. Pins corpses where they fall.

Cheap, fast, weak. Each hit pins the target to whatever's behind it for 4 seconds — so a stunned Tank against a wall is yours. Ammo is just scrap (the new resource from the other brief), bridging gunner and engineer playstyles.

CHAIN TASER

One bolt. Up to five zombies. Twitching.

Fires a single electric arc that chains to 4 nearest enemies within 5 tiles of the previous link. Heavy stagger, mid damage. Drinks battery cells (rare loot). Perfect against dense, low‑HP packs — useless on a lone Tank.

KATANA

No ammo. No noise. One clean arc.

Wide horizontal swing hits up to 3 enemies in a 90° cone. High base damage, silent (no aggro pull from neighbors), but only Steel & Mythic chest drops. Charged swing (hold attack 1.2s) executes any non‑boss zombie below 40% HP — finisher animation, half‑second i‑frames.

Pairs with the Scout perk lane: silent boots + silent steel = a corridor of dead walkers and no one ever knew.

SLEDGEHAMMER

Knockback. And it breaks weak walls — yours or theirs.

Slow swing, huge damage, 2‑tile knockback on hit. Resets crowd pressure when you're cornered. Also doubles as a terrain tool — three hits on a wood wall and it's rubble. Use against barricaded POIs, or against your own base when you change layouts.

CHAINSAW

Pull the cord. Burn the fuel. Wade in.

Hold trigger to spin — continuous DoT on whatever it touches, ignores armor. Drinks fuel at 1 unit/sec (same fuel that runs the power grid and vehicles). When fuel hits zero it's a dull club. Idle noise pulls aggro from the entire screen.

RIOT SHIELD

Offhand slot. Eats damage from the front 180°.

Equip in the new offhand slot alongside any one‑hand weapon (pistol, machete). Blocks 100% of frontal melee, 60% of frontal ranged, until it breaks (300 HP). Shield bash on right‑click: knockback + stagger, 8s cooldown. You can't sprint with it raised.

SCOPES & SIGHTS

Red‑dot. 4× scope. Thermal. Pick your problem.

Three sight tiers, each attachable to rifle / crossbow / railgun. Red‑dot halves hipfire spread. 4× scope doubles effective range, narrows view cone (you see less around you — real cost). Thermal outlines zombies through fog and smoke; useless on Tanks (cold) — that's the wrinkle.

Drops as a flat attachment item. Drag onto a weapon in inventory to apply. One sight per gun.

HOOKSview radiusfog/weatherinventory

SUPPRESSORS

A muzzle device that buys you 4 more seconds.

Cuts the weapon's aggro radius to 20%. Costs you 15% damage and adds a small spread penalty. Critical for night runs and sewer dungeons. Wears out after 200 rounds — they're disposable, like real ones.

HOOKSaggro radiusdurability

DRUM MAGS

Double the bullets. Half the reload speed.

Capacity 2×. Reload time 1.6×. Fits SMG, AR, shotgun, minigun. Great for fixed defenses (turret feed, hold‑a‑hallway plays); bad for ambush runs. Quickdraw mag is the opposite — 0.6× capacity, 0.5× reload — same slot.

HOOKSreloadturrets

UNDERBARREL

Flashlight, laser, or a second muzzle.

Three options for the under‑slot. Tac‑light projects a 5‑tile cone of vision at night; blinds Shamblers (1s stagger). Laser sight –30% hipfire spread, but draws aggro from anything that sees the dot. Foregrip: –20% recoil for the heavy guns.

INCENDIARY ROUNDS

Every bullet a tiny flamer tick.

Slot into pistol, AR, shotgun. On hit: a 4‑second burn DoT (1.5 dmg/tick) plus a 1‑tile ground patch that lingers 2s. Stacks with the Flamethrower's existing DoT. Useless in rainstorms — pairs the ammo system with the weather idea cleanly.

ARMOR‑PIERCING

One bullet, three corpses behind it.

Pierces up to 3 targets in a line, full damage on the first, 70% / 40% behind. Cuts straight through a Tank's armor stat — the only reliable counter besides explosives. Strongly themed to rifle / sniper play.

Rare blue‑tip ammo crates spawn near military POIs (locker rooms, crashed APCs).

EXPLOSIVE ROUNDS

A grenade where the bullet hits.

Each shot detonates a small AoE on impact (1.5‑tile radius, 50% of bullet damage as splash). Friendly fire on. Will pop barrels, of course. Rarest tier — 4 rounds per pickup, mythic chests only.

AMMO PRESS

Workbench upgrade — print your own rounds.

A craftable add‑on to the workbench. Convert scrap + spent casings (a new pickup zombies sometimes drop after firefights) into rounds. Standard ammo is cheap; the exotic types above cost 5× and need a rare component (capacitors, thermite, white phosphorus jars from labs).

NAMED WEAPONS

"The Widowmaker." "Old Reliable." "Coyote."

Mythic‑chest tier drops are uniquely named instances of a base weapon, each with one rolled trait from a pool: +25% headshot crit, ricochets once, reloads while holstered, refunds 1 bullet on kill. Names persist in the death screen — "killed by a horde, holding The Widowmaker."

Cheap to author (drop a trait table, a name table); huge identity payoff per run.

HOOKSchest tiersdeath screenleaderboard
E·02
L
WEAPON MASTERY

Each gun earns its own tiny perk tree.

Per‑weapon kill counter. Every 50 kills with the pistol unlocks a node on its 3‑node mastery — recoil cut, mag +2, exec finisher. Same for every weapon class. Encourages the player to commit to a build rather than pad‑swapping.

Survives death only if you're holding the gun when you die — light meta progression with stakes.

HOOKSkill counterperksdeath penalty

JAMS & WEAR

A condition meter. A panic clear‑and‑rack.

Every shot wears the weapon. At 30% condition, jam chance rises sharply. Jam = next trigger pull just clicks; tap R twice to clear (1.5s). At 0% the weapon is junk — strip it for parts at a workbench. Adds texture; not punitive (only firearms, not melee).

HOOKSworkbenchcondition stat

THE WALL

A base prop where every gun you've ever found hangs.

Craft a Weapon Wall at your safehouse. Any firearm you've picked up gets a slot. Click to instantly swap your loadout to that weapon, ammo included. Lets a long run actually use the arsenal it's hoarded — and turns your base into a trophy room.
