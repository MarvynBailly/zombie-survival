Driving School

THE LINE

  • Momentum is everything. The car keeps its velocity — lift off the throttle and steer to rotate the chassis before a corner.
  • Handbrake = drift. Tap Space mid-corner to break rear grip. The car slides, you keep speed, smoke pours off the tires.
  • Nitro on tap. Drifting and clean laps fill the boost meter. Hit J / Shift for an overtake burst — but the meter drains fast.
  • Stay on tarmac. Grass and gravel cut your grip and top speed. Clip apexes, don't plow the verge.
  • Run a season. Championship strings circuits together — finishing position scores points toward the title.
